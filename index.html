About RentPesa.

RentPesa is a digital property management platform for landlords and tenants looking to rent out properties on daily, weekly or monthly basis. Rentpesa offers a platform that connects tenants to property owners, enables the tenants to pay via MPESA, VISA, MasterCard or Bank Transfers for Short Term or Long Term Rentals while taking over the maintenance and management of the property on behalf of landlords.



Through Rentpesa, both the tenant and landlord can access their rent statements online for purposes of reconciliation while the landlord can also set up a % of their rental income invested in Treasury Bills and Treasury Bonds through our investment partner, African Diaspora Asset Managers (ADAM) that is Licensed by the Capital Management Authority. Moreover, tenants can make daily contributions towards their monthly rent by transferring small amounts daily or weekly to their Rentpesa Wallet

Put up your property for Monthly Rental Management by the only platform that does not Lock out Non-Card booking clients

We collect your Monthly rent from your tenants through Bank Transfer, MPESA, VISA or Mastercard Your tenants will receive their Monthly rental and invoice through their phone and email

You will get your own portal page to view and reconcile all the rent collected on your behalf

You can set up a % of your collected rental income to be invested through our partners ADAM Fund Managers so that your money continues to grow even as we collect more rent for you

We also take charge of the maintenance of your property to ensure its in the best shape at all times !
